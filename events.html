Schedule Events with Us!

To schedule an event with us, reach out to guidingeyes@cornell.edu with details of your event (organization, date, time, brief description)

  • Our fundraising chair will get back to you as soon as possible, letting you know if we can attend.
  • You reserve the room.
  • We coordinate the dogs.
  • Pricing: Generally $40/hour or a portion of donations if it is a charity event
